How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from within the app by following these steps:

  • Open MovieScout and make sure you are logged in
  • Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation bar
  • Scroll to the bottom of the profile screen
  • Tap Delete Account below the Log Out button
  • Confirm twice when prompted — your account is permanently deleted immediately
2

What Gets Deleted

The following data is permanently removed when you delete your account:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Watch history, wishlist, and ratings
  • Comments on movies and TV shows
  • Custom lists and saved collections
  • Follower and following relationships
  • All sent and received recommendations

This action cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

3

Deletion Timeline

Account and cloud data deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation. Any residual data in backup systems is fully purged within 30 days.

4

Need Help?

If you are unable to access your account or need us to manually process a deletion request, contact us and we will handle it within 7 days.
